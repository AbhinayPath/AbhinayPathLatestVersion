---

## 3. Technical SEO

### 3.1 Sitemap Implementation
- Dynamic sitemap.ts generating all URLs
- Priority weighting: Core pages (1.0-0.95), City pages (0.85-0.8)
- Change frequency: Daily for workshops/auditions, Weekly for others

### 3.2 Robots.txt
- Allow all core content pages
- Block API routes and auth pages
- Sitemap reference included

### 3.3 Performance Optimizations
- Server-side rendering for SEO-critical pages
- Image optimization with Next.js Image component
- Lazy loading for below-fold content

## 4. Off-Page SEO Strategy

### 4.1 Backlink Building

**Target Sources:**
1. **Theatre Publications:** The Hindu Metro Plus, Times of India Entertainment, Indian Express Lifestyle
2. **Theatre Portals:** Theatre forums, drama school websites
3. **Educational Institutions:** NSD, FTII, drama colleges
4. **Cultural Organizations:** Sangeet Natak Akademi, state cultural departments
5. **Event Listing Sites:** BookMyShow (events section), Eventbrite India

**Outreach Tactics:**
- Guest articles on theatre blogs
- Press releases for major workshops/festivals
- Partnerships with theatre schools for cross-promotion
- Interviews with featured artists

### 4.2 Social Signals

**Platforms to Focus:**
- Instagram (primary - visual content)
- Facebook (groups & events)
- YouTube (workshop previews, artist interviews)
- LinkedIn (professional networking)
- Twitter (industry news)

**Content Calendar:**
- Daily: Audition/workshop updates
- Weekly: Artist spotlights, festival highlights
- Monthly: Industry insights, career tips

### 4.3 Local SEO

**Google Business Profile:**
- Create/optimize profile with category "Performing Arts"
- Add all service areas (cities)
- Regular posts about upcoming events
- Collect reviews from workshop participants

**Local Citations:**
- JustDial
- Sulekha

- Yellow Pages India
